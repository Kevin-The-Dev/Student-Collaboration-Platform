C. K. Pithawala College of Engineering and Technology is a self financed Institute that started its functioning in December, 1998. The institute is affiliated to Gujarat Technological University, Ahmedabad. The institute is managed by the Navyug Vidyabhavan Trust that was registered under Bombay Public Trust Act 1950 as a Public Education Trust on 21 February 1965.
